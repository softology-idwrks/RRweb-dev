TurboView Console

The Features and Functions on the TurboView Control Console

The TurboView Control Console appears whenever you SINGLE CLICK while RapidReading. This will PAUSE RapidReader. The TurboView window will display the last word you read. The mouse cursor will appear and you can then access the Tools and Controls without having to return to ParagraphView.

 
USING THE TURBOVIEW CONTROLS

Rewind a Paragraph

Click to REWIND to top of previous paragraph

Back a Line
Click to REWIND to the beginning of the sentence

Stop

Reveals the ParagraphView

Play

Resumes from the word in the TurboView Window

Forward a Paragraph

Click to move to the beginning of the next paragraph

Set Speed

Click on the top or bottom arrow to increase/decrease speed by 25 WPM each click

Create a User-Note

Click to call the Note Pad to attach a comment to the current word displayed in the TurboView Window

Create a Dog-Ear

Click to place a Dog-Ear marker at the current word in the TurboView Window

Highlite Text

Click to return to the ParagraphView in Highlite Mode

Save/Save As RapidReader .r2d file
Click to open the "Save" Dialog Box

Call the Help Files

Click to access the Help menu
