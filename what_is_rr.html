What is RapidReader?


The RapidReader Difference


The problem of reading faster

R

apidReader is a software developed from well researched science for immediately accelerating reading speed on a computer screen. The basis is a method that's been studied for more than 20 years called Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP). The essence of the method used to achieve high reading speed is to convert the text into a movie like experience. This presentation eliminates the need for eye movement and scrolling.

 

While there have been previous attempts to use this format for computer reading in the past, studies have shown that using RSVP is only truly efficient for very short periods. The basic presentation of metronomic word flashing was fatiguing and unpleasant for the reader beyond a few minutes. * RapidReader's patented technology changed all of that by focusing on true usability for the reader in the real world. This means ergonomic comfort and ease as well as speed.

RapidReader's Patented Human Cadence™ Display Makes All the Difference. The Rhythms of Natural Speech

The real difference is RapidReader's patented "Human Cadence" display. Rather than simply showing the text on the screen, RapidReader analyses the file and the words are then streamed using the rhythm and timing of human conversation. The result is naturalness, comfort and improved comprehension even at very high speeds. From more than 10 years of research and feedback from thousands of customers the display of text that you see with RapidReader provides a level of comfort and usability that's unique. All of this with a "lean back" experience that's as easy and automatic as watching a movie.
