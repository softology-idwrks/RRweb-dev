See What Reading 500 WPM is Like and Then Test Your Own Comprehension.

PLACEHOLDER TEXT:

We're not going to make any claims about how "amazed" you'll be by how much you can actually comprehend at very high reading speeds. Everyone's expectations are different when it comes to reading speed. We will say however that thousands of RapidReader users comfortably read between 350 and 950 words per minute depending on the material. They control their own reading speed while reading PDF files, web pages, long stories and articles and ebooks. Whatever they need to read. Simply by sitting back and watching. So can you.

Average reading speed on paper: Between 150-250 words per minute
Average reading speed on a computer (with scrolling): 100-130 words per minute
Average reading speed on a hand held or mobile device: 40 words per minute

ABOUT THE VIDEOS

The videos are some samples of what it's like to read at 500 wpm. These of course are just videos and because they're pre-recorded you won't be able to change or control the speed the way you would with RapidReader. So It may take a moment or two to get used to. But what you're about to see are actual screen caps of RapidReader in action. You'll see the patented cadencing rhythm of the word display that contributes so much to comprehension and comfort. The videos haven't been modified or enhanced. In fact the display is "You Tube" standard size. Considerably smaller than RapidReader actually is on a computer and about the same as on a mobile phone.

After each of these short videos there are a few questions for you so you can see how much you actually "got".. Some of them are more difficult than others. The "trick" is to not "try to read it" but rather sit back and watch it. You already know what the words mean all you really have to do is recognize them as you would any kind of symbol. Just let the video do the work of displaying them to you.

When you're watching please be aware that you're not scrolling, you can lean back and watch while the computer does the work for you. And then think about how much you understood of what you were reading. Read a couple of these and you'll have no doubt that you are able to comprehend at very high speeds. As you'll see, the limit to your reading speed is not about understanding the symbols you're seeing. It's about the efficiency of the way you've had to read in the past. It's a matter of displaying the words in a way that helps you overcome the barriers to reading speed.

This isn't voodoo science or a novelty app It's a technology born of years of real science and development that will allow you to change the way and amount of information you can easily consume.
