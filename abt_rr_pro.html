About RapidReader PRO


It's not voodoo science or a novelty app...
RapidReader PRO is a Serious Tool for Serious Reading

PDF is the most commonly used file type for information of all kinds...


Whether you're a professional, in business or in school, you run into them everywhere. PDFs can also be among the most time consuming and laborious documents to read. RapidReader PRO solves that problem with a new kind of software tool for fast effortless reading, saving you hours of time in the process.



All you have to do is "drag and drop" the PDF onto the RapidReader desktop icon. RapidReader PRO uniquely analyzes the contents of the PDF and extracts the text. Then its patented technology displays it for you enabling you to read it many times faster with the ease and comfort of seeing a movie.

All you have to do is sit back and let the software do the work for you. It's that simple.



It also works in Microsoft Outlook for instantly reading whole batches of emails and attachments at hundreds of words per minute. All with just one click. It's ideal for catching up in record time, reviewing a correspondence history or for sharing information.



After more than 12 years of research and development RapidReader was created specifically for this purpose.
